Privacy Policy

How Manjahi Dev's collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Manjahi Dev's (operated by Wilson Mburu Karanja) collects, uses, and safeguards information you provide when you visit this website or use our contact form.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit the contact form on this website, we collect the following information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Company or organisation name (optional)
  • Service interest and project description
  • Budget range and preferred contact method

We do not collect this information automatically through cookies or tracking pixels beyond standard web analytics (if enabled).

2. How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through our contact form is used solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry or project brief
  • Communicate about services, timelines, and project details
  • Provide the service you have requested

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Contact form submissions are processed through Formspree (a third-party form service) and delivered to our email. Formspree's own privacy policy governs how they handle form data in transit. We store client communications in standard, password-protected email and do not maintain an independent database of visitor submissions.

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Links

This website may contain links to external sites (LinkedIn, GitHub, WhatsApp). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies.

5. Analytics

If Google Analytics or similar tools are enabled on this site, they may collect anonymised data such as page views, browser type, and approximate location. This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you. You may opt out via your browser's privacy settings or tools such as the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information you have provided to us. To exercise these rights, contact us at wmkaranja996@gmail.com.
